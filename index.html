About us

For a long time, Licost remains one of the market leaders in the distribution of fruits and vegetables. Our significant working experience and partner network ensure the best quality of the delivered products and optimal prices. Quality control is an essential part of our daily operations, which make Licost a trustworthy company, recognised on the territory of the European Union and Russian Federation. Among our customers are some large chain retailers such as Monetka (Ltd Element-Trade) and Magnit (JSC Tander). At the moment we are negotiation cooperation with Auchan Holding and X5 Retail Group.

Our expertise

Logistics
Licost ensures excellent service, punctually delivering goods in time. Container shipments are accomplished by Maersk and MSC, world leaders in cargo services.
Distribution
International expertise and the team of specialists are the factors which bring our customers' experience to the next level. Our spread distribution network grants first-class product quality, optimal delivery time, and solutions that will suit your business requirements.
Packing
We use modern equipment for wrapping products in a variety of different packings. We offer tailored solutions to preserve and present your goods, depending on your order and needs. No matter if you need to pack just two lemons or ten kilos of potatoes, we will deliver the highest quality product.
